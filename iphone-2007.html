iPhone (2007)

The iPhone was released in 2007 and was the first generation of iPhone.

The iPhone model released in 2007 was the iPhone (1st generation). It was announced by Steve Jobs, the co-founder and CEO of Apple, on January 9, 2007, and was released to the public on June 29 , 2007.
