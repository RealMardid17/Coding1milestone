iPhone 3G

The iPhone 3G was released in 2008 and was the second generation of iPhone.

The iPhone 3G, released in 2008, was a significant upgrade to the original iPhone. It introduced 3G connectivity, GPS, and iOS 2.0, which added support for third-party apps. With a sleeker design and improved performance, the iPhone 3G was a major step forward in the evolution.
