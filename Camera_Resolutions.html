iPhone 5

The iPhone 5, released in 2012, features a 4-inch IPS LCD multi-touch touchscreen display with a 16:9 aspect ratio, a 8-megapixel autofocus camera, and Apple's A6 processor. It supports up to iOS 10 and was available in 16GB, 32GB, and 64GB models. The device was released in black or white and was available in several countries on September 21, 2012.
