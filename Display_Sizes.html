iPhone 4

The iPhone 4, released in 2010, features a 3.5-inch IPS LCD multi-touch touchscreen display, a 5-megapixel autofocus camera, and Apple's A4 processor. It supports up to iOS 7 and was available in 16GB and 32GB models, priced at $199 and $299 respectively. The device was released in black or white and was available in several countries on June 24, 2010.
